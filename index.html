About us

The Flower Workshop is a national flower and gift delivery service across Ukraine. We have been engaged in the delivery of flowers and gifts for more than 10 years and have a lot of experience in organizing congratulations at a high level! We also understand that our work is not only the delivery of flowers, but primarily the delivery of a good mood, the transfer of your emotions to dear people at a distance! A bouquet of flowers is a wonderful greeting for the celebration! But in order to please a dear and loved one, it is not necessary to look for an excuse - give happiness and positive emotions today! We are always happy to help you make someone happier!
